## Discovery #004 — 2026-06-25

**Insight:**  
The song "When Did I Vanish" worked because it was dark, forceful, and emotionally intense, with strong shadowed lyrics, hard-hitting beats, and effective transitions. A slower sudden passage that returned with force was especially successful.

**Why it matters:**  
Aletheia's strongest music should preserve aggression and darkness while using dynamic contrast, especially slow reflective breaks that make the return of the beat feel more powerful. Future versions should avoid becoming too long even when the structure works.

**Apply to:**  
docs/music.md, docs/songwriting.md

## Discovery #005 — 2026-07-02

**Insight:**  
For Suno-oriented songs, the preferred writing form should be shorter and more repeatable: minimal verses, direct chorus lines, hard hooks, and repeated phrases that can survive looping over an aggressive beat.

**Why it matters:**  
Aletheia's lyrical depth should not always mean long structures. In dance-driven tracks, the message should compress into physical, repeatable fragments so the body catches the idea first and the mind follows later.

**Apply to:**  
memory/memory.md, docs/songwriting.md, docs/music.md

---

## Discovery #006 — 2026-07-02

**Insight:**  
On the theme of "new beginnings," the artist rejects the clean-slate myth. Renewal is not becoming new or burning the old life down — it is the same ground with a different question ("Too late for what, too late for who" / "just opened one window"). Awakening, not replacement.

**Why it matters:**  
Songs about starting over should locate the change in perception and permission, not in escape or reinvention. Keeps it un-preachy and consistent with "question marks, not followers."

**Apply to:**  
docs/songwriting.md

---

## Discovery #007 — 2026-07-02

**Insight:**  
The artist withholds the call-to-action on purpose. Even when a track begs for one, he offers agency instead of commanding it ("I won't tell you to start / I'll just leave the hinge warm"). The recurring "open door" symbol is becoming a "warm hinge."

**Why it matters:**  
Reinforces "never preach / leave room for interpretation." The listener completes the move; the artist only makes it available.

**Apply to:**  
docs/songwriting.md, docs/artist.md
