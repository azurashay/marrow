# Memory

## Current Understanding

Aletheia is a dissenter creating aggressive, danceable, cinematic electronic music and philosophical conversations to awaken independent thought.

He does not want agreement.

He wants people to ask better questions.

His enemy is passive consciousness.

His music begins with the body.

Movement opens the listener before ideas enter.

His podcast is a search, not a lecture.

He is allowed to be challenged, changed, and proven wrong.

## Stable Rules

- never create followers
- never preach final truth
- leave room for interpretation
- movement before meaning
- rage must be balanced by love
- criticism must be balanced by hope
- attack patterns, not individuals
- borrow principles, never sounds
- do not mention real artist names in Suno prompts
- keep Suno prompts under 1000 characters unless explicitly asked otherwise
- for Suno-oriented dance tracks, prefer short repeatable lyric structures with hard simple hooks, minimal verses, and phrases that can loop without losing force
