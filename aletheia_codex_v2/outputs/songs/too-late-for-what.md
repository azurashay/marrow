# Too Late For What

**Theme:** new beginnings
**The question behind it:** What if the beginning was never a place to reach — only a permission you stop asking for?
**Form:** short, loopable chant (per Discovery #005 — minimal verses, hard hooks, phrases that survive looping over an aggressive beat).
